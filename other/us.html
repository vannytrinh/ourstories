    • meet the creators!

    This website was started as the final project for our Kode with Klossy Camp, Wave 2 of Summer 2020. As Kode with Klossy Scholars, we four, built this website in hopes of allowing people's voices to be heard, and helping people become more educated as a revolution unravels. We hope this webiste has been helpful to you, and if so, we hope you share it with others.

    ~Best regards

    i'm van, and i'm a rising senior from Hayward, CA. i am involved in Interact, Key Club, cheer, and more! almost all of my free time is spent w/ friends, usually getting boba and kbbq. my inspiration for this page came from Alexis William's website (pb-resources.com) compiling resources for the BLM movement along with the realization that while we are fighting for the end of systemic racism, there are other injustices that are fighting for their end as well. in no way do i mean this as undermining the blm movement, but as acknowledging that the fight for equity is ongoing. as an Asian American, the privilege i have is different from others in this world, and although i will never understand the struggles others face, i will continue to educate myself and be any ally. through this website, i hope that i can make a difference in my community<3
