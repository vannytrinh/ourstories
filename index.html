    about us vocab ways to help

    • We understand that publicly sharing your stories can be hard. Nonetheless, your story is valuable, and it deserves to be heard. We hope that our platform provides an oppurtunity for people talk about their experiences with injustices in this world, as we continue to fight for changes to be made. Through our platform, we also hope to educate others and provide resources so everyone can continue to educate themselves, and change our current system.

    We acknowledge that information is continuously updating, and new resources are constantly coming to light. If you feel that information resources should be added, or updated, please reach out to us through email (ourstories.help@gmail.com) or Instagram (@ourstories.website). Although we may not be the most knowledgable on these topics, we hope to help by compiling resources as we continue to educate ourselves. Thank you for taking the time to visit this website!
