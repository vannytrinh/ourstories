    • Black Lives Matter Movement

    The Black Lives Matter movement was created in 2013 after George Zimmerman, the killer of an innocent 17 year old boy named Trayvon Martin, was unjustifiably acquitted of all charges. Recently, the brutal murder of George Floyd sparked recent protests, and the Black Lives Matter movement has been recieving increasing widespread attention across the world. Throughout history, African Americans have always been systematically oppressed. The Black Lives Matter Movement aims to get justice for victims of police brutality, and end institutionalized racism, once and for all.
