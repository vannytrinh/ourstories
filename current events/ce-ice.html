    • ICE Detention Centers

    Many medical professionals have warned government officials of the danger of these ICE detention centers as COVID-19 continues to rapidly infect millions. Many have stated the detainees should who are at risk should be released immediately as conditions in the centers themselves are unsanitary. To combat COVID-19 many detention centers are spraying chemicals that they claim are safe but many agencies have state dthat these chemicals are toxic and immigrants are having allergic reactions to such substances. Additionally,"the EPA states that the use of fumigation or wide-area spraying to control COVID-19… Fumigation and wide-area spraying are not appropriate.”
